About the song

Originally written by singer-songwriter Jimmy Webb, the song talks about a particular soul incarnating in four different places. The song was influenced by the real life highwayman Jonathan Wild.

About the band

The Highwaymen were an American country music supergroup consisting of Johnny Cash, Waylon Jennings, Willie Nelson and Kris Kristofferson. The naming came from the very same song when Glenn Campbell played it in front of all 4 of them. They thought the name The Highwaymen was fitting since they were always on the road.
